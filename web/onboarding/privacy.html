Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This Privacy Policy explains how Trust App collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our web app, Android APK, or related services.

Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone, and login identifiers.
  • Donation data: amount, receipt details, payment status, and timestamps.
  • Device data: browser type, IP address, and basic analytics events.

Payments

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store card numbers, CVV, or UPI PINs. Razorpay provides us with payment IDs and status updates required for receipts.

How we use your data

  • To create receipts and show donation history.
  • To confirm payment status and prevent fraud.
  • To provide support and respond to queries.
  • To improve product performance and reliability.

Data sharing

We share information only with service providers required to operate the app, such as Razorpay for payments and hosting providers for service availability. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Donation records are retained for compliance and accounting. You can request account deletion by contacting support, subject to legal retention requirements.

Security

We use access controls, encryption in transit, and secure hosting to protect your data.

Children

Our services are not intended for users under 18.

Contact

For privacy questions, email us at support@trust-app.in.
