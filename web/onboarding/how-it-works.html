Donation flow

How Trust App works

From the moment you choose an amount to the final receipt.

Member sign in

You log in with your member account to access donations.

Order creation

Our server creates a Razorpay order for your selected amount.

Secure checkout

Razorpay checkout collects payment details on their secure page.

Verification

We verify the payment signature and confirm the payment status.

Receipt and status

Receipt is generated and your donation status updates in the app.

Where funds settle

All payments settle to the Trust bank account linked in Razorpay. The Trust handles spending and compliance for donations.

Receipts and records

Instant receipt

Receipts are available right after confirmation.

Donation history

You can view past donations from your account history.

Support ready

Contact support for payment issues or missing receipts.
