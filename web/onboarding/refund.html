Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

Donations are generally non-refundable because they are used for Trust activities. However, we will review refund requests for duplicate payments or technical errors.

Eligible cases

  • Duplicate payment for the same donation.
  • Payment captured but donation not reflected in the app.
  • Payment marked failed but money debited from your account.

How to request a refund

Email jayaprakashnagaraj333@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction with: donation amount, payment ID, and a brief description of the issue.

Processing time

Approved refunds are processed within 7 to 10 business days, depending on your bank.

Cancellations

Donations cannot be canceled after the payment is completed. If the payment is not completed or fails, no charge will be captured.
